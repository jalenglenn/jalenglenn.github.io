About Me


I am passionate about exploring the possibilities of our rapidly changing media landscape by contextualizing such changes in a historical narrative that can help guide us in discerning how to capitalize on the opportunities of the future. Although my undergraduate study was focused on film, I am also passionate about other forms of media such as television, radio, social media, and print/digital journalism.
